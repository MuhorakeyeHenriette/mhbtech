About MHBTech

MHBTech builds clear, maintainable web examples and small apps that demonstrate modern development practices. Our goal is to make it simple for developers to learn and ship.

About This System

This demo site runs on a small Node.js + Express server. Pages are static HTML under the pages/ folder and include small client-side enhancements (forms, search, and navigation).

  • Static pages: `home`, `about`, `contact`, `login`
  • Simple Express server for serving pages
  • Responsive, accessible design
